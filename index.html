Welcome to Card Battle Stadium!

You and your opponent start out with 250HP.

You will attack using some specialized cards that deal damage to your opponent.

To attack your opponent, click on the "Fight" button

The fight button will pull out a random card from the deck.

If a 10 card is pulled out, it deals 10 damage.

If a 50 card is pulled out, it deals 50 damage.

Sometimes, a heal card may appear, which heals yourself.

All heal cards start with +.

Sometimes, a mystery card may appear, which deals a random amount of damage.

The higher the class of the mystery card, the more likely it will deal more damage.

Rarely, a critical hit may land, dealing more damage.

Rarely, the card may crumble, which doesn't cause any damage except to the card itself.

You may quit the battle when it's your turn by pressing the "Quit" button.

If you lose all your HP, you lose the battle, and the opponent wins.

If the opponent loses all their HP, you win the battle, and the opponent loses.
